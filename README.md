An RFM segmentation is a method of analyzing customer behavior based on recency, frequency, and monetary value. It can be used to identify a company's best and most loyal customers, as well as customers that the company is at risk of losing. In this project, I used SQL and Tableau to conduct a simple RFM analysis based on the Tableau Superstore sample data. I wrote a query in SQL to categorize each customer on a scale of 1-3 for each of the three factors, and then I exported that data to Tableau. Once in Tableau, I created some graphics displaying the data, such as counts of the customers in each segment, average monetary spend by segment, total spend by customer, and RFM scores by customer.
